## Science Fiction

### Isaac Asimov
- **Bio:** Russian-American writer and biochemistry professor
- **Influenced by:** Jules Verne, H.G. Wells, Eando Binder
- **Influenced:** Orson Scott Card, Dan Simmons, the entire field of robotics

**Works:**
- Foundation series (1951-1993)
- I, Robot (1950)
- The Caves of Steel (1954)

### Ursula K. Le Guin
- **Bio:** American author, "the greatest American writer of her generation"
- **Influenced by:** Lao Tzu, Tolkien, feminism, anthropology
- **Influenced:** NK Jemisin, Ted Chiang, Becky Chambers

**Works:**
- The Left Hand of Darkness (1969)
- The Dispossessed (1974)
- Earthsea series (1968-2001)

### Ted Chiang
- **Bio:** American speculative fiction writer
- **Influenced by:** Ursula K. Le Guin, Borges, linguistics, philosophy
- **Influenced:** Contemporary SF heavily

**Works:**
- Stories of Your Life and Others (2002)
- Exhalation (2019)
